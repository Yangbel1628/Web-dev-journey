Notice Boards

  • Quality Education: The School is renowned for its expertise in education. It provides a learning environment that combines clinical exposure, community engagement, seminars, and classroom teaching. Students gain practical skills through externships and guided tours to reputable foundations.

  • Infrastructure: The School boasts well-furnished chemistry labs with certified technicians. All classrooms are equipped with state-of-the-art facilities and software to enhance the learning experience.

  • Vision: To impart knowledge in allied sciences, enabling students to meet global requirements and challenges.

  • Mission: To provide innovative education with modern technology, effective pedagogy, and industry collaborations. The School nurtures creativity by incorporating both classic and innovative allied science concepts.
